Botijo, ceramic air cooler design


A background in industrial design has helped shape the way I think through problems and present solutions. Botijo is a sustainable proposal to cool inner spaces taking advantage of ceramic properties.

Botijo is spanish for a porous clay container, meant to keep water fresh. The material porosity allows stored water to filter and evaporate in contact with the environment, having a continuous cooling effect. The design maximizes the cooling effect with flower shaped inner walls, aided by a fan to expel cooled air. In order to keep the cooling process, water must be added to the container cavity.

Academic project displayed at the Metropolitan Design Centre of Buenos Aires, 2015.

Tools used: Rhinoceros, Keyshot, PS, AI
