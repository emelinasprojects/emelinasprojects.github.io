About me





I'm a conversation designer working on designing, training and improving AI assistants. Focused on making UX and data analysis work together to deliver meaningful conversational experiences while maximizing the technical possibilities of the tool at hand.

Conversation Design

Working with chatbots for the last few years and having received training on the Conversation Design Institute framework, I design user-focused AI assistants to meet business goals.

UX

Training in UX UI provided skills and methods to break down user journeys and motivations, making the user persona a central piece of any digital experience.

Customer Service

As a former customer advisor, I've acquired a strong awareness in user behavior as well as a clear understanding of contact center operations and needs.

Analytics

I monitor AI assistants' conversation metrics and KPIs, handling user-bot interaction data to continually improve performance.

Design processes

With a background in Industrial Design, I have experience on teams and as a freelancer across various design projects, honing skills in problem-solving, project research, and visual communication.
